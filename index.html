How we met


We both started their Ph.Ds in the summer of 2015 at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill and enrolled in the same statistics course. Kimiko went in to office hours to check her homework answers. Joe went in to office hours to figure out how to start the homework. He ended up getting more help from his future wife than the teaching assistant. By the end of the next summer, we were spending every day together. We discovered that they both enjoyed the outdoors, filling the next year with days spent hiking, mountain biking, camping, and cultivated a love for rock climbing. When Kimiko traveled with Joe to the Bahamas to help with his field research, Joe realized he wanted to be with her forever. Kimiko knew Joe was the one after a spontaneous backpacking trip to chase the solar eclipse in the Appalachians. A year later, he asked her to be his wife on top of a mountain while snowboarding in Vail, Colorado.

We are extremely excited to see where our life takes us after we defend our Ph.Ds this spring!
