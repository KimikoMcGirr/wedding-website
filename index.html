How we met


They both started their Ph.Ds in the summer of 2015 at the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill and enrolled in the same statistics course. Kimiko went in to office hours to check her homework answers. Joe went in to office hours to figure out how to start the homework. He ended up getting more help from his future wife than the teaching assistant. By the end of the next summer, they were spending every day together. They found out that they both enjoyed the outdoors, filling the next year with days spent hiking, mountain biking, camping, and discovering a love for rock climbing. Kimiko traveled with Joe to the Bahamas to help with his field research, where Joe realized he wanted to be with Kimiko forever. Kimiko knew Joe was the one after a spontaneous backpacking trip to chase the solar eclipse in the Appalachians. A year later, he asked her to be his wife on top of a mountain while snowboarding in Vail, Colorado.

After defending their Ph.Ds this spring, they will be moving out to Davis, California to start a new adventure on the West Coast as newly weds!
